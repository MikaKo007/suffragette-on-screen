About this database
Titles follow the spelling recorded in contemporary British trade press sources, principally Kinematograph Weekly and The Bioscope. Where modern film databases (IMDb, BFI, Wikidata etc.) record variant spellings — including differences in singular/plural forms, articles, or punctuation — these are noted in the Alt Titles field. Such discrepancies reflect the fluid titling conventions of the silent era rather than errors in either source.

The database covers 138 films (including one title identified in two separate productions: No.32 Pathé Frères and No.32b Lubin). All entries are verified against primary sources. Synopses are drawn from Kinematograph Weekly, The Bioscope, IMDb, and BFI where available.

Verification status: data cross-referenced against IMDb, BFI, Wikidata, and primary trade press sources. "Discrepancy found" entries contain conflicting information between sources. This database is a work in progress; corrections and additions are ongoing.
